Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

1. Introduction

Welcome to FrameIt, a product of Odd Omens LLC. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and use our web application.

2. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries, questions, or concerns, please contact us at support@oddomens.com. Your satisfaction and peace of mind are our top priorities.

3. Data Collection

Important: FrameIt does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or the content you create. All your mockups, images, and settings are stored locally in your browser using localStorage.

The FrameIt website has basic tracking through a self-hosted version of Plausible Analytics. Information is generalized and doesn't capture any identifiable information. The information collected includes:

  • Country
  • Page visits
  • Browser type
  • Device type

We only gather personalized data that you willingly provide through our contact forms or support submissions.

4. Types of Data Collected

Non-personalized information through website analytics:

  • Country location
  • Page visit counts
  • Browser used
  • Device type

Personalized information only through direct contact:

  • Name: To personalize interactions and correspondence
  • Email Address: To respond to inquiries and keep you updated

5. Data Usage

In the interest of transparency, we want to emphasize that FrameIt does not employ cookies or similar tracking technologies to gather information about your activities within the application. Furthermore, we abstain from data collection for advertising or analytical purposes within the application itself.

All mockups, images, text layers, and settings you create in FrameIt are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your creations
  • Your data is private and secure
  • Clearing your browser data will remove your saved mockups

6. Third-Party Tools

FrameIt uses the following third-party services:

  • Plausible Analytics: Self-hosted, privacy-friendly analytics for our website (not the application)
  • Google Mail: Used for email communications with users who reach out for support

7. Analytics

The FrameIt website captures general data such as country location and page visits, without tying the two together. No personal or device-specific analytic data is captured within the application itself.

8. User Consent

Given our data collection practices, there's no requirement for users to provide consent within FrameIt. Rest assured, your information is only collected when you voluntarily submit it through our contact forms or email.

9. Data Storage and Retention

To bolster your confidence in FrameIt and Odd Omens LLC, it's vital to recognize that we do not retain any user-generated content or personal data on our servers. All application data is stored locally in your browser. Consequently, there's no data stored on our servers, eliminating any concerns about data security or privacy breaches related to your mockups and creations.

10. Compliance with Data Protection Laws

Our conscientious approach to data collection ensures that we comply with relevant data protection laws including GDPR and CCPA. Since we limit our data collection to names and email addresses provided voluntarily by users, and store no user-generated content on our servers, there are minimal compliance requirements. However, we continuously review and update our practices to adhere to new regulations.

11. Privacy Policy Updates

To remain informed about any changes or refinements to our privacy policy, we encourage users to periodically visit this page. Transparency is pivotal to our commitment to privacy. Any significant changes will be communicated through our website or via email if applicable.

12. Age Restriction

FrameIt is accessible to users of all ages, and there are no age restrictions in place. However, we encourage guardians to monitor the usage of our application by minors to ensure a safe experience.

13. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data through the application, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. If you've contacted us via email, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal information we may have
  • Request deletion of your contact information
  • Opt-out of any communications

14. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries, questions, or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@oddomens.com. Your satisfaction and peace of mind are our top priorities.

15. Legal Disclaimers

This privacy policy adheres to standard legal disclaimers and terms and conditions to ensure clarity and fairness in our interactions with users.
