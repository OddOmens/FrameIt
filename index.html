Welcome to FrameIt

Getting Started

Create beautiful designs with or without images! Add images by clicking "Add Image" or drag & drop files directly onto the canvas. You can also create stunning text and background-only designs.

Background Options

Choose from solid colors, gradients, or upload custom background images. Use the "Random" button to quickly try different backgrounds.

Noise & Texture

Add realistic textures to enhance your backgrounds:

  • Types: Paper, canvas, grain, Perlin noise, static, fabric, and dot patterns
  • Intensity: Control how strong the texture appears
  • Opacity: Adjust transparency of the texture overlay
  • Blend Modes: Choose how texture interacts with background
  • Scale: Make texture patterns larger or smaller
  • Invert: Reverse texture colors for different effects

Image Controls

Fine-tune your mockups with corner radius, padding, rotation, flipping, and advanced shadow settings including color, opacity, blur, and positioning.

Text Layers

Add custom text overlays with full typography control, positioning, shadows, backgrounds, and layering options.

Watermarks

Add your logo or watermark to protect your designs:

  • Upload: Click or drag to upload PNG/JPG watermark images
  • Transparency: PNG files with transparency work best
  • Positioning: Choose from 5 preset positions
  • Opacity: Adjust transparency from 0-100%
  • Size: Scale from 10% to 200% of original size

Resolutions

Choose from preset resolutions optimized for social media platforms or create custom dimensions.

Shortcuts

  • Ctrl/Cmd + Z: Undo
  • Ctrl/Cmd + Y: Redo
  • Ctrl/Cmd + Scroll: Zoom
  • Drag: Pan canvas when zoomed

Export

Export individual mockups or batch export all images with custom formats (PNG, JPEG, WebP) and quality settings.
