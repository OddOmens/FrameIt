Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FrameIt

Is FrameIt really free?

Yes! FrameIt is completely free to use with unlimited exports and no watermarks. No credit card required.

What image formats does FrameIt support?

FrameIt supports PNG, JPEG, WebP, and other major image formats. Upload any screenshot or design and export in high-quality formats.

Can I use FrameIt mockups commercially?

Absolutely! Use your FrameIt mockups for app stores, client presentations, portfolios, and marketing materials without restrictions.

Do I need to download any software?

No downloads needed! FrameIt runs entirely in your web browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile.

What's the maximum image size I can upload?

Upload images up to 10MB in size. FrameIt exports your mockups in high resolution up to 4K quality.

Are my mockup projects saved?

Yes! Your mockup projects are automatically saved in your browser, so you can return and edit them anytime.
