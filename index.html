Frequently Asked Questions

Is FrameIt really free?

Yes! FrameIt is completely free to use with unlimited exports and no watermarks. No credit card required.

We support all major image formats including PNG, JPEG, WebP, and more. You can also export in multiple formats.

Can I use FrameIt for commercial projects?

Absolutely! You can use FrameIt for personal and commercial projects without any restrictions.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required! FrameIt works entirely in your browser on any device.

You can upload images up to 10MB and export in resolutions up to 4K for crystal clear results.

Your work is automatically saved in your browser, so you can continue where you left off anytime.
